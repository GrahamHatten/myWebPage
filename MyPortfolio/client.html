Web Security

As you use the internet, it's important to be aware of the potential security risks and how to protect yourself. Here are some tips:

  • Use strong, unique passwords for each of your accounts.
  • Keep your software up to date to protect against security vulnerabilities.
  • Be cautious of emails, links, and downloads from unknown sources.
